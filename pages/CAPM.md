- [Capital Asset Pricing Model](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Capital_asset_pricing_model)
- $E(R_i) = R_f + \beta_{i}(E(R_m) - R_f)$
	- **where:**
		- *ERi* = expected return of investment
		- *Rf* = risk-free rate
		- *βi* = [[beta]] of the investment
		- (*ERm* − *Rf*) = market risk premium
