- a format for data in which each column is a variable, and a column is added for the frequency of the given combination of variables.
- e.g.:
	- | Foo | Bar | Freq |
	  | --- | --- | --- |
	  | 1 | 1 | 5 |
	  | 1 | 2 | 2 |
	  | 2 | 1 | 6 |
	  | 2 | 2 | 0 |
	  | ... | ... | ... |
