- a measure of the strength of [[relationship (stats)]] between two continuous random variables.
	- bounded between -1 and 1! this makes it useful for understanding strength of linear relationship
- symbols:
	- **ρ_xy** for populations, **r_xy** for samples
- can be calculated with:
	- the [[Pearson product-moment coefficient of correlation]]
- in [[R]], we can use `rplot()` to see a correlation matrix
