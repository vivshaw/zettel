- a special case of a [[binomial distribution]] with only a single trial.
	- can be written $X \sim Bern(p)$
	- only relevant parameter is the probability $p$
	- it's [[discrete]]
- formulas:
	- [[expectation]]: $p$
	- [[variance]]: $p (1 - p)$
	- [[support]] $0$ and $1$
	- [[PMF]]: $P(X = x) = p^x (1-p)^{(1-x)}$
