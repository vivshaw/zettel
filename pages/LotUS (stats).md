- the **Law of the Unthinking Statistician** states that, for any function $g(X)$ of a random variable $X$, its [[expectation]] is:
	- for a discrete distribution, $E[g(X)] = \sum_{x} g(x) p_x(x)$, where $p_x$ is the [[PMF]]
	- for a continuous distribution, $E[g(X)] = \int_{-\infty}^{\infty} g(x) f(x) dx$, where $f(x)$ is the [[PDF]]
