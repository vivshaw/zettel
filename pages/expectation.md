- calculated $E(X) = \sum_i x_i P(X = x_i)$
	- to say it a different way, sum each value in the [[support]] multiplied by their probability
- expectation is a linear operator! so $E(X + Y) = E(X) +E(Y)$, and $E(aX) = aE(X)$
- related to [[variance]] like so: $Var(X) = E(X^2) - (E(X))^2$
